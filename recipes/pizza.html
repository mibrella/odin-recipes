Pizza recipe

Description

We will made some neapolitanien like pizza. Which you can customize to your likings by adding toppings.

Ingredience

  • 300g of all purpos flour
  • 2g Yeast
  • 140g of water
  • salt
  • tomato sauce
  • spice you like
  • toppings after your liking
