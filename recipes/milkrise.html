Milk Rise Recipe

Description

This will be so delicious your friends will aks you for the recipe. we combine some simple ingredience to create something phenomenal greate. You wont get enougth of it.

Ingredience

  • 1l Milk
  • 300g of milk rise
  • 50g Sugar
  • 1 Vanilla slice
  • 1Tp Salt

Steps

  1. Grap a big bowl and add the Milk and heat it until it starts to rise
  2. Add the rise and the sugar.
  3. Season with salt and vanilla
  4. Cook until the rise is soft and tender.
  5. Enjoy!!!
